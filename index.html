Welcome

I explore the formation and evolution of galaxies through cosmic time.

I am currently an assistant research faculty (former post-doctoral researcher and SNSF post-doctoral fellow) at the Infrared Processing and Analysis Center (IPAC) at Caltech. My main interests include early galaxy formation at very high redshifts and the physics of star-formation quenching in massive galaxies at intermediate redshifts.
